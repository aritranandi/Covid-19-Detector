Covid Guidelines

The coronavirus that causes COVID-19 spreads through droplets containing the virus that can enter another person’s body through their eyes, nose, mouth, or lungs. Therefore maintain a physical distance of at least 6 feet from people around you to avoid spread. Get properly vaccinated. Do not forget to wear a mask every time you get out of your house even if your vaccination shots are completed. Wash your hands frequently and use an alcohol-based hand sanitizer. Use germ-free disinfectants to keep your surroundings clean. Whenever you cough or sneeze, use a tissue or a handkerchief to avoid the droplets from spreading. Ensure that they are properly disposed. In case you develop symptoms of being COVID positive, get yourself tested without delay and opt for home isolation if the condition is not severe.
